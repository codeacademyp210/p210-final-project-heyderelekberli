About Movie

Name: Point Blank

Production: 2019 - USA

Duration: 86 Min.

Genre: Action , Thriller

Director: Joe Lynch

Starring: Anthony Mackie , Marc McGiver , Frank Grillo , Marcia Gay Harden , Teyonah Parris

IMDB: 5.7 / 10

Paul is a rational and orderly emergency room nurse with an ordinary life. Another beautiful reason for Paul's life is that he and his wife are pregnant. Very happy for her soon-to-be-born babies, Paul's orderly and calm life is intertwined with a mess. His pregnant wife was kidnapped and taken as collateral. Determined to save the life of his wife and unborn baby, Paul is forced to cooperate with a criminal who is seriously injured and suspected of murder. Embarking on a deadly battle between rival gang members and corrupt policemen, the two have to put aside their differences and work together to survive. Their lives will depend on their trust in their partnership.
